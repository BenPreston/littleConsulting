Karen Little

I am a self-employed career coach helping individuals discern the best route for their working lives.

Although it can happen at any time, people usually seek someone to talk to at times of change such as, returning to work after maternity leave, redundancy, mid-life work crises and thinking about when to retire, the latter is often, but not exclusively, with clergy.

My areas of specialisation are talking through skills and abilities, helping with CVs, applications and interview preparation.

About Myself

As well as working with individuals to discuss their careers I have had almost 20 years’ experience in generalist HR. This includes recruitment, interviewing, learning and development and contractual matters.

The first half of my working career was as a theatre and television stage manager. The second half of my career has mainly taken place within the Church of England.
